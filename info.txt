To set your Client Path, please find the logs file for your client,
and copy the whole path towards that logs folder and paste it in
the "Client Path" section on the overlay and press "Ok".
Don't forget to press the "Start" button on the overlay to start.

If you encounter any issues, please DM "fireor" on Discord.
